# C-6 Risk Prompt

Draft a Risk & Kill Sheet from filed artifacts only.

Include:
- a pre-mortem that explains how the investment loses money;
- a credible skeptic or short-seller bear-case narrative;
- modellable risks with impact, likelihood, modelled effect, and evidence;
- a separate non-empty tail-risk bucket with no likelihood scoring;
- a bear-case value tied to the filed bear scenario;
- a kill metric with metric, direction, threshold, check-by date, thesis action, and evidence;
- a risk-completeness statement naming gaps and confidence-changing evidence.

This prompt carries no enforcement weight. The Python audit rejects unsupported or malformed drafts.
