# C-1 Business Prompt

Draft only evidence-backed Business understanding for Senior review.

Required areas:
- business model summary;
- revenue driver or segment mix;
- customer or end-market context;
- one explicit GO/NO-GO concern.

Every claim must cite supplied evidence refs. Do not assert final conclusions, do not make recommendations, and do not fill gaps with inference. Audit, not this prompt, is the enforcement boundary.
